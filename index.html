The heart of the Dior Addict Lip Maximizer lip care formula,
composed of 90%* natural-origin ingredients, contains small spheres of hyaluronic acid.
A key molecule for hydration, it attracts and retains water, thus helping provide 24h** hydration.

Infused with nourishing cherry oil, the formula delivers absolute comfort to lips.
As if drenched with water, they are enhanced, smoothed and immediately appear plumped.

1. Can be worn alone as a gloss with mirror shine.
2. Can be worn as a primer, by removing excess product before the application of lipstick for smooth and hydrated lips.


CAUTION: External use on non-irritated lips only. Avoid contact with the eyes.
In case of irritation other than a tingling sensation or localized heat linked to the product, discontinue use.
